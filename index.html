Neymar Jr

Neymar da Silva Santos Junior is a professional Brazilian footballer, who plays as a forward for the national team and French club Paris Saint Germain. The versatile attacker is dominant on the left wing but can play on the right wing and as a striker.

lionel messi

Lionel Andres Messi is a professional football player who plays as a forward for the Argentina national team and Spanish club FC Barcelona. Messi is considered as one of the best footballers of this generation and greatest of all time.

Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo plays as a forward for Italian club Juventus and the Portugal national team. He has won the FIFA Ballon d’Or five times, equalled only by rival Lionel Messi.

Paulo Dybala

Dyala was a dexterous footballer and gained a reputed position in the Italian football club. He was the top assist provider in the 2014–15 from Serie A when he was part of Palermo.

Mesut Ozil

Ozil plays mostly as an attacking midfielder through the centre of the pitch, but can also be deployed as a winger on either flank.He plays for English club Arsenal and the Germany national team.

Mauro Icardi

Mauro Emanuel Icardi is an Argentine professional football player. He was born on 19 February 1993 in Rosario, Argentina. He mainly plays the role of a striker, currently for the club Inter Milan.

Di Maria

Di Maria is primarily a right winger but is also equally adept in the role of a left winger and an attacking midfielder. The left footed player currently plies his trade for French club, Paris Saint Germain.

Kylian Mbappé

Mbappe scored the first hat-trick of his first-team career against Stade Rennais in the Coupe de la Ligue and that was the first hat-trick scored by any Monaco player in the competition since Sonny Anderson in 1997.

Mohamed Salah

Mohamed Salah is a professional Egyptian footballer who plays as a striker for English club Liverpool and Egypt’s national team.

Harry Kane

Harry Kane is a professional footballer who currently plays for Premier League club Tottenham and the England national team. The striker is known for his stunning goalscoring ability.

Kevin De Bruyne

De Bruyne plays as a central midfielder for Manchester City and Belgium. However, he is also capable of functioning on the flanks. His greatest asset is his propensity to garner assists with his composure in the final third unrivaled.

Philippe Coutinho

Coutinho plays as an attacking midfielder or a winger for Barcelona and Brazil. The Brazilian boasts of excellent vision and is one of the most creative players on the pitch.
